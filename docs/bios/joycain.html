Joy Cain

Board Member - President

Joy Cain

I am so very glad to be anxiously engaged in a good cause with such a wonderful board, a host of amazing volunteers and some very talented performers!

Joy Cain is the founding member of Center Place Christian Theatre. She directed her first mini-musical, Fish Tales in 2005 at Center Place Restoration School. Since then directed 10 musicals for CPRS, including Annie, Disney's Beauty & the Beast, The Music Man, The Sound of Music, The King & I, Meet Me in St. Louis, Cinderella, Annie, Disney's The Little Mermaid, Jr,and Disney's Peter Pan, Jr. She was the assistant director for Center Place Christian Theatre's first production of Seven Brides for Seven Brothers. She has directed the 3 most recent CPCT summer shows: Fiddler on the Roof, OKLAHOMA!, and Little Women. She had the pleasure of teaching 4th grade at CPRS for 11 years, but returned to the practice of Speech-Language Pathology.
