Auditions for The Sound of Music

Who?
Children 5 & up, older youth, young professionals, and young marrieds
When?
Friday, May 24th from 6:30 pm til 8:30 pm
Saturday, May 25th from 10:00 am til 12:00 pm
Friday, May 31st from 6:30 pm til 8:30 pm
Saturday, June 1st from 10:00 am til 12:00 pm
CALL-BACKS: Saturday, June 1st from 1:00 pm til 4:00 pm
Where?
Living Hope Restoration Branch
2425 1/2 Crysler Ave, Independence, MO
Format?
Prepare 16 measures of music, not from this show. No recorded music please. Bring marked copy of your music for the provided accompanist. There will be cold reads, no monologues required, and dance auditions. Wear comfortable shoes. There are non-singing and non-dancing roles available.

Mission

Center Place Christian Theatre (CPCT) is committed to producing quality, family friendly entertainment, while providing an uplifting environment and experience for its participants.

Our Theme Scripture: "When ye are in the service of your fellow beings, ye are only in the service of your God." -Mosiah 1:49

This effort is a service, a means of bringing others joy. Audience members may have burdens, but performers use their God-given talents, to transport them to another place and time, where concerns are temporarily forgotten.
