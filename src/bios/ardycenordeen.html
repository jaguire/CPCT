Ardyce Nordeen

Ardyce is a veteran theatre bug, who began making up plays for the neighborhood kids to act out when she was in elementary school. Her love of being onstage continued through high school and college, but her college training was in teaching. Her thirty-two year teaching career included eighteen years as a music teacher, which allowed for quite a few stage performances with children from three to eighteen. Nine of those years were at Center Place Restoration School, where she directed the yearly drama performance and sometimes wrote the scripts.

Ardyce is married to Craig Nordeen and they have three grown children, all married, and six grandchildren, with a seventh on the way, due this fall. The acting/singing bug has infected most of them, too.
