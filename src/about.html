Our Mission

Center Place Christian Theatre (CPCT) is committed to producing quality, family friendly entertainment, while providing an uplifting environment and experience for its participants.

 

  • History
  • Benefits

CPCT was "born" out of the desire of CPRS alumni to have a summer musical, after attending the 2nd annual CPRS musical, Beauty & the Beast. The CPRS fall musical program started after they had graduated from CPRS and they asked for the opportunity.

CPCT's name was chosen by CPCT members, to honor Center Place Restoration School as the origin of the group, but changed from its original name (Center Place Alumni Theatre Group) to be inviting to those who were not CPRS students.

CPCT's membership consists of those who love Jesus and the restored gospel.  Participants sign a contract, that clearly delineate the expectation Christ-like behavior and interactions, displaying Christian morals and values.

Ages and number of participants vary depending on the activity, but are typically high school students through older youth/young marrieds/etc.  We have open auditions, which are evaluated and scored by a casting committee. Elementary students are invited, if needed for the show being produced.

  • CPCT Is a bridge building, unifying entity, that promotes charity & good-will between various groups of believers.
  • CPCT provides members an opportunity to be anxiously engaged in a good cause, being a positive example & providing a service to the community.
  • CPCT provides members opportunities to use their God given gifts & to magnify their talents in service to each other, and to the community.
  • CPCT is an organization that values its participants, by working with their summer schedules, excusing them to attend &/or staff church camps, reunions or caravans.
  • CPCT members volunteer their time to serve and educate students at CPRS with the fall musical program as members of the production team.
  • CPCT members volunteer their time to serve at various camps by teaching drama classes and/or directing vignettes/skits for worship & campfires.
  • CPCT board members are all volunteers who donate their time to assisting in this cause.
